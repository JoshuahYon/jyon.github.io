My name is Joshuah Yon. (an uncommon last name right?)

The story behind my last name begins . . .

My family is originally from a small village in Guangzhou, China. During a time of distress and uncertainty, my great grandparents decided to go to this new area called America. However, it was no easy journey to get there. They boarded a ship that brought them to America,, but had to change our last name to "Yon" to seem less asian as tensions against immigration were growing in America. My great grandparents were able to start a new life in the Chinatown of Chicago.

Fastforward a few decades . . .

My grandparents tried their best to better our life and generations to come. They worked very laborous jobs to provide for their family (Note:This is on my Dad's side). When my dad was around 30, my grandpa began building a house that we could collect rent on when it was finished. A year or so later, it was finished and they decided this was going to be their new home. To this vey day, I living in the same house that he built.
